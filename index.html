What is Cubed?

Cubed is an off-the-shelf payment solution for merchants looking to offer their customers a crypto payments option, with white label solutions also available.

Cubed builds payment processing software for cryptocurrency adoption in both physical brick and mortar businesses and for ecommerce. This includes point of sale software, web checkout components, payment APIs, consumer wallets, and custom block explorers, with integrations to popular inventory management and CRM software.

What is EDEN?

EDEN is a supply chain visibility toolset for any industry.

Multichain Ventures is developing the EPCIS compliant EDEN Blockchain to provide an immutable, decentralized ledger for supply chain visibility. The EDEN blockchain is fundamentally industry agnostic and can serve to ensure the providence of goods ranging from agriculture to technology to high end consumer goods.

What is Reli.cloud?

Reli is a multi-cloud DevOps platform that provides development teams with open-source tools to automate and scale their blockchain infrastructure.

Reli.cloud combines cutting edge open-source technologies into an accessible Platform as a Service, automating DevOps needs and application publishing with high availability. Leveraging Kubernetes, Reli is multi-cloud, running on infrastructure including Amazon Web Services, Google Cloud Platform, and more. Reli enables teams to quickly deploy a number of software products, including blockchain infrastructure, with easy scalability.
